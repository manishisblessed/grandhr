## GrandHR — Delete your account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your GrandHR
account and personal data in two ways.

### Option 1 — In-app (recommended)

1. Open the GrandHR mobile app.
2. Go to **Settings → Danger zone → Delete my account**.
3. Re-enter your password and type **DELETE** to confirm.

Your account, support history, and session data are deleted
immediately. You will be signed out.

### Option 2 — From the web

If you can no longer sign in (e.g. lost device), email
**privacy@grandhr.in** from the address associated with your account
and include:

- Your full name.
- Your employee ID (if known).
- Your Company's name.
- A short statement that you want to delete your GrandHR account.

We will reply within 72 hours, verify your identity, and complete
deletion within **30 days**.

### What gets deleted

- Your profile, contact details, and login credentials.
- Your support tickets and in-app chat history.
- All active sessions across all devices.

### What may be retained

- **Employment records** your employer is legally required to keep
  (payroll, tax, contracts, attendance logs). GrandHR stores these on
  your employer's behalf; they are governed by your employer's data
  policy and local employment law.
- **De-identified aggregate analytics** that cannot be traced back to
  you.
