## Full description (Play Store — 4,000 chars max)

```
GrandHR brings your workplace HR into your pocket.

For employees:
• Clock in and out with one tap, view your attendance history.
• Apply for leave, track balances and approval status.
• Access payslips and company documents anywhere, any time.
• Chat with your HR team, raise support tickets, get quick answers from the HR assistant.

For HR administrators:
• Approve leave and manage employee records on the go.
• View live dashboards of attendance and headcount.
• Send announcements and respond to tickets.
• Run everyday HR automations.

Built for teams of any size, from startups to global enterprises.

Security & privacy
• End-to-end encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+).
• Session tokens stored in the device's secure keystore.
• Optional biometric app lock (Face ID / fingerprint).
• Auto sign-out after inactivity.
• No ads. No location tracking. No selling your data.

You will need an account provisioned by your employer. Contact your
HR administrator if you need access.

Questions or feedback? Email support@grandhr.in.
```
